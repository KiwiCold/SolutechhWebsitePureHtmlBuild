How this runs
This quiz runs entirely in your browser because there's no backend. The instrument list, the correct answers, and the scoring logic all live in the page itself, which means anyone who opens devtools or view-source can see the answer key directly.

That's a known and accepted tradeoff, not an oversight: keeping everything client-side means no data leaves your browser, and it can work offline if you load the page. This is a self-study tool, not a locked-down exam treat your score accordingly.
