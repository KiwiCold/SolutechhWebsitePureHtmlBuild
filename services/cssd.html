About CSSD Solutions

The Central Sterile Supply Department (CSSD) is a vital unit in hospitals responsible for cleaning, sterilizing, and distributing medical instruments. It ensures all equipment used in patient care is safe, sterile, and infection-free.

CSSD Gallery

Key Features

Decontamination

Cleaning and disinfecting used instruments to remove harmful contaminants.

Assembly & Packaging

Inspecting and preparing instruments for sterilization using proper packaging.

Sterilization

Using advanced methods like steam, gas, and plasma to eliminate microorganisms.

Storage & Distribution

Safe storage and timely supply of sterile instruments across departments.
