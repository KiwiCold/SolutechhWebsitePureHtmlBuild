Digital Health Programme Initiative

Structured Preventive Health & NCD Risk Management

About the Digital Health Programme

In collaboration with our strategic partner, Solutechh supports public health bodies with a structured preventive health approach. This initiative is designed to assist in the early identification and prevention of lifestyle-associated non-communicable diseases (NCDs) such as Stroke, Heart Attack, Diabetes, Kidney Disease, and Cancer. By transitioning from isolated screenings to systematic, repeatable care pathways, the program aims to strengthen mass-population healthcare delivery.

Key Features of the Initiative

Structured Assessments

Combines routine biometrics (blood pressure, blood sugar, cholesterol, BMI) with lifestyle and neuro-cardio-metabolic risk profiling.

Validated Risk Insights

Utilizes tools recognized in global cardiovascular and stroke prevention guidelines to identify high-risk individuals early.

Frontline Worker Support

Assists clinical teams and community health workers with standardized assessment workflows and automated reporting tools.

Data Security & Compliance

Ensures patient confidentiality and local data compliance by hosting information securely on approved national health servers.

Why Implement this Preventive Mechanism

Integrates into existing public health infrastructure (PHCs, CHCs, and health centers) without creating parallel workflows.
Shifts public health efforts from single-visit screenings to actionable, risk-based follow-up care.
Provides de-identified, program-level aggregate risk intelligence to support strategic public health planning.
Helps reduce long-term healthcare operational costs through systematic early-intervention strategies.
